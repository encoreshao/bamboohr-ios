Frequently Asked Questions

How do I configure my BambooHR account settings?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Account Configuration. Enter your company domain, employee ID, and API key. The app will automatically validate your credentials and sync your data.

Why is my data not syncing?

Check your internet connection and ensure your API key is valid. If the issue persists, try logging out and back in, or contact your BambooHR administrator for assistance.

How do I track my work hours?

Navigate to the Time Entry tab. Select your project, add time entries for each day, and submit your timesheet. You can also view your weekly and monthly summaries.

Can I request time off through the app?

Yes! Go to the Leave tab and tap "Request Time Off". Fill in the details, select the dates, and submit your request for approval by your manager.

How do I change my employee ID?

In Settings ‚Üí Account Configuration, update your Employee ID. The app will automatically clear cached data and refresh with your new information.

Troubleshooting

Having technical issues? Try these common solutions:

  • Ensure you have the latest version of the app installed
  • Check your internet connection and BambooHR server status
  • Verify your API key and employee credentials are correct
  • Clear app cache by changing and reverting your employee ID
  • Force close and restart the app
  • Restart your device if issues persist
